Service Line

Hauler Safety & Compliance

We audit your fleet's safety infrastructure, connect your telematics and camera data into automated workflows, and generate insurance-ready compliance reports using AI.

The Problem

Your cameras are recording. Nobody's watching.

Most haulers have cameras, telematics, and driver logs. But the data sits in silos. Nobody is turning it into something your insurance carrier can see.

That means you're paying premiums as if you have no safety program at all. We fix that.

What haulers are leaving on the table

  • Camera systems installed but no compliance reporting
  • Telematics data collected but never analyzed
  • Driver logs completed but not aggregated
  • Insurance renewals with no safety evidence presented
  • Manual safety reviews that happen once a year (or never)

What We Build

Automated safety and compliance workflows

We connect your existing hardware and data sources into a single pipeline that produces actionable compliance output.

Camera & DVR Audit

Physical or remote assessment of camera placement, coverage gaps, DVR health, storage retention, and video quality across your fleet.

Telematics Dashboard

Custom dashboard that ingests GPS, hard braking, speeding, idle time, and driver behavior data. Produces weekly safety scorecards automatically.

AI Compliance Reports

Local LLM reads your safety data and generates plain-English compliance summaries mapped to what insurance carriers care about. Regenerate monthly.

Safety Program Design

We design a documented safety program with pre/post-trip checklists, incident protocols, and training tracking that meets carrier requirements.

Premium Reduction Strategy

We package your compliance report and safety program into a presentation your broker can take directly to underwriters to negotiate lower rates.

Sensor & Hardware Integration

Tim evaluates your existing camera and sensor hardware, recommends upgrades where needed, and ensures everything feeds into the compliance pipeline.
