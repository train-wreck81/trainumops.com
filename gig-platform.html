Gig Services Platform

AI-powered document generation and gig delivery for property managers. Submit a request, get a legally compliant PDF, delivered to the door with photo proof. End to end in under 24 hours.

How It Works

Submit. Generate. Deliver. Prove.

The entire pipeline is automated. Five intake methods. AI-generated documents. Gig runner delivery with photo proof, GPS, and timestamp.

01

PM Submits Request

Via natural language, screenshot upload, document upload, structured form, or Zillow URL. Five methods, zero friction.

02

AI Parses & Generates

Claude extracts fields, generates a legally compliant PDF per FL Statute § 83.56(3). End-to-end in ~4 seconds.

03

Printed & Dispatched

PDF sent to print, gig runner dispatched. Real-time status tracking through the entire pipeline.

04

Delivered with Proof

Notice posted on door. Photo proof + GPS + timestamp captured. Complete delivery record for your files.
