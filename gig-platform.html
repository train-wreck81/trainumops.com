Product

Gig Services Platform

AI-powered document generation and gig delivery for property managers. Submit a request, get a legally compliant PDF, delivered to the tenant's door with photo proof. End to end in under 24 hours.

How It Works

Submit. Generate. Deliver. Prove.

The entire pipeline is automated. You submit an order through any of five intake methods. AI generates a legally compliant document. A gig runner delivers it with photo proof, GPS, and timestamp.

01

Property Manager Submits Request

Via natural language text, screenshot upload from AppFolio/Buildium, document upload, structured form, or Zillow URL. Five intake methods, zero friction.

02

AI Parses & Generates Document

Claude extracts tenant name, address, county, amount owed. Generates a legally compliant PDF per FL Statute § 83.56(3). End-to-end in ~4 seconds.

03

Document Printed & Dispatched

PDF sent to print, gig runner dispatched to the property. Real-time status tracking through the entire pipeline.

04

Delivered with Proof

Runner posts notice on tenant's door. Photo proof + GPS coordinates + timestamp captured. You get a complete delivery record for your files.

Five Ways to Submit

Meet property managers where they are

Natural Language

Type or paste plain text like "Evict John Smith at 456 Oak, Orlando, $1850 March rent." AI parses and extracts all fields.

Screenshot Upload

Upload a screenshot from AppFolio, Buildium, QuickBooks. Claude Vision reads the image and extracts tenant name, address, amount owed.

Document Upload

Upload a lease, invoice, or rent ledger PDF. AI extracts relevant fields automatically.

Web Form

Submit structured fields directly: tenant name, address, county, amount, landlord info. Fastest path, no AI parsing needed.

URL Scrape

Paste a Zillow link for open house flyers. Scraper pulls property details automatically.
